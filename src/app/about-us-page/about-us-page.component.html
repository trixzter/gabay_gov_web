WHO WE ARE

GABAYGOV IS A WEB-BASED PLATFORM DEDICATED TO ENHANCING PUBLIC AWARENESS AND PARTICIPATION IN GOVERNMENT-SPONSORED EVENTS. WE STRIVE TO BRIDGE THE GAP BETWEEN LOCAL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND RESIDENTS BY PROVIDING A CENTRALIZED, USER-FRIENDLY PORTAL WHERE PEOPLE CAN DISCOVER, REGISTER FOR, AND STAY UPDATED ON VARIOUS COMMUNITY PROGRAMS.

OUR MISSION

OUR MISSION IS TO EMPOWER COMMUNITIES BY MAKING IMPORTANT GOVERNMENT SERVICES MORE ACCESSIBLE. WE BELIEVE THAT EVERY RESIDENT SHOULD HAVE AN EASY WAY TO STAY INFORMED ABOUT INITIATIVES SUCH AS FREE MEDICAL CHECKUPS, DENTAL SERVICES, "LIBRENG TULI," "LIBRENG PAGUPIT," AND OTHER BENEFICIAL PROGRAMS.
